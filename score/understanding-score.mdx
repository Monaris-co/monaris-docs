## What the Monaris Score is

The **Monaris Score** is a **0–1000** reputation score built from verified cashflow history. It is the central product of the platform: every other product either feeds the Score or is unlocked by it.

**Tagline:** Your cashflow history, turned into a verifiable financial identity.

---

## Current implementation (live)

- **Range:** 0–1000
- **Tiers:** Tier C (0–510), Tier B (510–850), Tier A (850–1000)
- **Trigger:** +20 points per cleared invoice (automatic)
- **Display:** Score with progress bar toward the next tier
- **Unlock ladder:** Tier C → Tier B → Tier A. Credit terms are shown as **Coming soon** until Monaris Credit launches (see [Score tiers explained](/score/tiers-explained)).

---

## Tier labels in plain language

| Score range | Label | Meaning |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| 0–510 | Building | Establishing your cashflow history |
| 510–850 | Established | Consistent, verifiable payment patterns |
| 850–1000 | Trusted | Institutional-grade cashflow history |

---

## Score composition (V1 — on-chain only)

| Signal | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Invoice completion rate | High | % of issued invoices that get paid |
| Payment timeliness | High | % of invoices paid on or before due date |
| Income consistency | Medium | Regularity and predictability of inflows |
| Client diversity | Medium | Number of distinct paying counterparties |
| Volume history | Medium | Total verified volume over time |
| Account age | Low | Time since first Monaris activity |
| Repayment behavior | High (V2) | On-time repayment of Monaris Credit |

---

## Score composition (V2 — on-chain + off-chain)

All V1 signals, plus:

- Bank statement consistency (via Plaid or export)
- QuickBooks / accounting reconciliation
- Payroll receipt verification
- CEX balance history (via zkTLS proof)
- Behavioral patterns: churn, disputes, defaults

---

## Tier thresholds — Score and volume

Tiers require **both** a minimum Score **and** minimum volume. You cannot reach Tier B on points alone from a tiny volume. Volume minimums are enforced in the system.

| Tier | Score minimum | Invoice minimum | Volume minimum | Credit access |
|------|---------------|-----------------|----------------|---------------|
| Tier C | 0 | 1 invoice cleared | $0 | None |
| Tier B | 510 | 10 invoices cleared | $500 verified volume | Up to $5,000 credit |
| Tier A | 850 | 25 invoices cleared | $5,000 verified volume | Up to $50,000 credit |
| Tier A+ | Invite only | 50+ invoices | $25,000+ volume | Up to $250,000 credit |

## What you see in the app (Score explanation)

Every Score display should include:

1. **Current score** and tier label
2. **Progress bar** to next tier with exact points remaining
3. **Score breakdown** — which signals help and which need improvement
4. **What improves your score** — up to 3 specific, actionable recommendations from Mona
5. **Score history chart** (V2)

---

## How to improve your score

- **Clear invoices on time.** Each cleared invoice is +20 points. On-time payments strengthen payment timeliness.
- **Increase volume and history.** More verified volume and longer history improve income consistency and volume history.
- **Diversify clients.** More distinct paying counterparties helps client diversity.
- **Repay credit on time (V2).** When Monaris Credit is available, on-time repayment is a high-weight signal.

Mona can give you **specific** recommendations based on your data (e.g. “Clear 3 more invoices to reach Tier B”).
