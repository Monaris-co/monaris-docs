The Monaris Score has four tiers. Each tier has minimum Score, minimum invoice count, and minimum verified volume requirements. All three must be met — you cannot reach a tier on points alone.

The **Monaris Credit Algorithm (MCA)** calculates your Score from on-chain cashflow and, optionally, off-chain data (bank accounts, credit scores, accounting tools). Connecting off-chain sources can accelerate your progression through tiers.

## Who this is for

Anyone building their Monaris Score who wants to understand what each milestone unlocks and what it takes to get there.

## The four tiers

### Building (0–510)

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Score minimum | 0 |
| Invoice minimum | 1 invoice cleared |
| Volume minimum | $0 |

**What it means:** You are establishing your cashflow history. Every user starts here. The focus is on creating invoices, clearing payments, and building initial momentum.

**What you can access:** Monaris AR/AP, Private Pay, cashflow picture, Mona insights.

### Established (510–850)

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Score minimum | 510 |
| Invoice minimum | 10 invoices cleared |
| Volume minimum | $500 verified volume |

**What it means:** You have consistent, verifiable payment patterns. Your cashflow history is strong enough to start proving your financial reliability to others.

**What you can access:** Everything in Building, plus Score sharing, selective disclosure, credit pre-approval view. At Score 700+: Monaris Credit eligibility and Invoice Advance.

### Trusted (850–1000)

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Score minimum | 850 |
| Invoice minimum | 25 invoices cleared |
| Volume minimum | $5,000 verified volume |

**What it means:** Institutional-grade cashflow history. Your financial identity is deep enough for significant credit access and priority terms.

**What you can access:** Everything in Established, plus priority credit terms, Monaris BNPL full access, up to $50,000 credit.

### Verified+ (Invite only)

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Score minimum | Invite only |
| Invoice minimum | 50+ invoices cleared |
| Volume minimum | $25,000+ verified volume |

**What it means:** Top 1% of Monaris users. Elite cashflow history with the highest trust band in the system.

**What you can access:** Everything in Trusted, plus up to $250,000 credit, institutional credit routing, premium terms.

## Why volume minimums exist

A Score cannot be gamed by creating fake invoices for $1 each. The Score requires both points (earned through real behavior) AND volume (real money moving). Established requires $500 in verified settled volume. Trusted requires $5,000. This is non-negotiable and non-bypassable.

## The critical rule

**You cannot reach a tier on Score points alone.** A user with 510 points but only $13 in total volume does not qualify for Established. All three requirements — Score, invoices, and volume — must be met simultaneously.

## FAQ

**How long does it take to reach Established?**
It depends on your activity. A freelancer clearing 2–3 invoices per month could reach Established in 3–4 months. More frequent invoicing and higher volume can accelerate this.

**Can I skip tiers?**
No. Tiers are sequential. Every user starts at Building and progresses through the same ladder.

**What happens if my Score drops below a tier threshold?**
Your tier updates in real time. If your Score drops below 510, you move back to Building. If it recovers, you move back up — assuming volume and invoice requirements are still met.

**Is Verified+ available to everyone?**
Verified+ is invite-only. Meeting the minimum requirements makes you eligible, but access is at Monaris's discretion.
