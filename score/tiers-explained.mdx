## Overview

The Monaris Score has four tiers. Each tier has **minimum Score**, **minimum invoice count**, and **minimum verified volume**. All three must be met. Credit access and terms improve as you move up.

---

## Tier C (entry)

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|--------|
| Score minimum | 0 |
| Invoice minimum | 1 invoice cleared |
| Volume minimum | $0 |
| Credit access | None |

You start here. Clear your first invoice to begin building. No credit is available until you reach Tier B.

---

## Tier B

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|--------|
| Score minimum | 510 |
| Invoice minimum | 10 invoices cleared |
| Volume minimum | $500 verified volume |
| Credit access | Up to $5,000 |

At Tier B you become eligible for **Monaris Credit pre-approval signals** when available.

---

## Tier A

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|--------|
| Score minimum | 850 |
| Invoice minimum | 25 invoices cleared |
| Volume minimum | $5,000 verified volume |
| Credit access | Up to $50,000 |

Tier A represents a stronger risk profile and improved trust for future credit access.

---

## Tier A+

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|--------|
| Score minimum | Invite only |
| Invoice minimum | 50+ invoices |
| Volume minimum | $25,000+ volume |
| Credit access | Up to $250,000 |

Invite-only. Highest trust band in the Score system.

---

## Tier labels in plain language

| Score range | Label | Meaning |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| 0–510 | Building | Establishing your cashflow history |
| 510–850 | Established | Consistent, verifiable payment patterns |
| 850–1000 | Trusted | Institutional-grade cashflow history |

---

## Critical rule

**You cannot reach a tier on Score points alone.** A user with 510 points but only $13 in total volume does **not** qualify for Tier B. Volume minimums are enforced at the system level.
