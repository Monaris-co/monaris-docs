## The old model: collateral-only

In crypto, credit has mostly been **collateralized**: you lock more than you borrow (e.g. 150% LTV). Your **income** and **payment history** are irrelevant. That excludes anyone who has cashflow but not idle collateral.

---

## The new model: cashflow-backed

**Stablecoin credit** in the Monaris model is **underwritten by cashflow**, not (only) collateral:

1. **Verifiable history** — Monaris reads your inflows/outflows (on-chain, and in V2 off-chain with consent) and builds a Score.
2. **Eligibility** — Credit is offered only when Score, history length, and volume meet minimums (e.g. Score 700+, 90 days, $5K volume).
3. **Repayment from future inflows** — When you get paid (e.g. invoice cleared), Monaris deducts the scheduled repayment first; you receive the rest. No need to remember to pay manually.
4. **Score improves or declines** — On-time repayment raises your Score and can increase your limit; late or default lowers it and can freeze new credit.

---

## Why it works

- On-chain history is **tamper-proof** and **timestamped**. We don’t rely on self-reported income alone.
- **Invoice and payment behavior** are strong signals: they show who pays you and whether you pay on time.
- **Automated repayment** from inflows reduces forgetfulness and aligns repayment with actual cashflow.

---

## Limits and risks

- Credit is **not** available to everyone. It is gated by Score and history.
- Defaults are possible. We have a **default management** process (grace → late → at risk → default; see [Monaris Credit](/credit/monaris-credit)).
