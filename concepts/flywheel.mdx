## One platform, one Score

Monaris has **five products** under one brand: **AR/AP**, **Pay**, **Score**, **Credit**, and **Business**. Each can be used **standalone**. Each **feeds the Monaris Score**. The Score is the thread that connects everything and drives the flywheel.

---

## How the flywheel turns

1. **You use AR/AP or Pay** — You create invoices, get paid, or connect your wallet. We read your cashflow.
2. **Score builds** — Every cleared invoice, every categorized flow, every on-time payment (and later, off-chain data) improves or maintains your Score.
3. **Mona adds intelligence** — She comments on events, explains Score moves, and suggests next steps (e.g. "Clear 3 more invoices to reach Tier B").
4. **Credit unlocks (V2)** — When Score and history meet the bar, you get access to cashflow-backed credit. Repayment is automatic from future inflows.
5. **Repayment and behavior** — On-time repayment and continued good behavior **raise** Score and limits; late or default **lowers** them.
6. **Privacy throughout** — Payments through Monaris Pay are private by default. You can share only what you need (selective disclosure). Score and history stay under your control.
7. **Teams (V3)** — Business tier uses the same Score and policy engine at the organization level. Team Score and treasury controls compound with individual behavior.

---

## Why it compounds

- **More products → more data → better Score** — Using both AR/AP and Pay gives a fuller cashflow picture.
- **Better Score → credit access → more volume** — Credit lets you smooth cashflow; repayment from inflows keeps you in good standing.
- **Trust and reputation** — Score becomes a **portable identity** you can share with counterparties, lenders, or platforms without exposing raw data.

---

## The sequencing rule (reminder)

Settlement efficiency first → Verifiable history second → Underwriting third → Credit last → Privacy layer throughout. The flywheel respects this order: we don’t offer credit before we have verifiable history and Score.
