## How paying an invoice works

When someone sends you a Monaris invoice, you can view it and pay in a few steps. Payment goes through the **Privacy Router** by default — only you and the sender see the amount on the receipt.

---

## Step 1: Receive the invoice

You get the invoice by:

- **Monaris inbox** — if you have a Monaris account, it appears in your inbox.
- **Email** — you receive a link to open the invoice in the browser.

---

## Step 2: Open the invoice

Open the link or the invoice in your inbox. You see:

- Amount (in stablecoin, e.g. USDC)
- Sender
- Due date
- Description (if provided)

---

## Step 3: Pay the invoice

1. Click **Pay invoice** (or equivalent).
2. **Connect your wallet** or choose a saved wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or other supported EVM wallet).
3. Confirm the transaction in your wallet.

---

## Step 4: What happens when you pay

1. Payment is sent through the **Privacy Router** (private by default).
2. The sender receives the funds.
3. Invoice status becomes **Cleared**.
4. You and the sender each get a **private payment receipt** (visible only to you two).
5. The sender’s **Monaris Score** increases by **+20 points**.
6. **Mona** can send the sender a short message about the payment (e.g. on-time, first payment from you).

---

## Privacy

- By default, the payment is **private**. Counterparties and amounts are not exposed on the public chain in a readable way.
- A **proof-of-payment** is anchored on-chain so the payment is verifiable without revealing details to the public.
- You and the sender can both see the receipt in your Monaris accounts.

---

## If you don’t have a Monaris account

You can still pay via the link sent by email. You will connect your wallet on the payment page and complete the transaction. You can create a Monaris account later to see your payment history and receipts.
