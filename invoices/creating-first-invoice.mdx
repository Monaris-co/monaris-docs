## What Monaris AR/AP does

**Tagline:** Invoice intelligence that builds your financial reputation.

Monaris AR/AP replaces manual invoicing and spreadsheet reconciliation. You create, send, and track invoices. Every invoice you clear contributes to your **Monaris Score**. The product is live at **app.monaris.co**.

---

## Before you start

- You need a Monaris account (Google or email).
- You need the recipient’s **wallet address** or **email** so they can receive and pay the invoice.

---

## Step 1: Create the invoice

1. In the app, start **Create invoice** (or equivalent).
2. Fill in:
   - **Amount** — in your chosen stablecoin (e.g. USDC).
   - **Due date** — when payment is due.
   - **Description** — what the invoice is for (optional but recommended).
   - **Recipient** — wallet address or email. If you use email, the recipient gets a link to view and pay in Monaris.

---

## Step 2: Send the invoice

After you save or send, the invoice is **Issued**. The recipient sees it in their Monaris inbox (if they have an account) or via the email link.

---

## Step 3: Track status

Invoice status moves in order:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|--------|
| **Issued** | Sent, not yet opened by recipient |
| **Viewed** | Recipient opened the invoice |
| **Paid** | Recipient initiated payment |
| **Cleared** | Payment confirmed; funds received |

When an invoice is **Cleared**, you get **+20 points** on your Monaris Score automatically.

---

## After your first cleared invoice

- Your Score starts to build. Clear more invoices to move toward **Tier B** (and later **Tier A**).
- **Mona** can comment on the event (e.g. first invoice cleared, on-time payment).
- V1 enhancements (when available): Score explanation panel, volume and minimums per tier, recurring templates (V2), early payment financing (V2).
