## Problem

Stablecoins solved global settlement. They did not solve cashflow. Every protocol moves money now fast, cheap, global. But moving money is not the same as making it work.

400 million people earn in stablecoins. The moment a payment hits their wallet, it stops. That cashflow doesn't generate a credit history, doesn't build a financial identity, doesn't unlock capital against income they've already earned. No BNPL. No borrowing. No Score. Just a number sitting in a wallet doing nothing until the next invoice clears.

The problem is not the money. The problem is everything that should happen after it arrives.

---

## The Solution

**Monaris sees it.**

We built the layer that makes **stablecoin cashflow actually work**. Every invoice, every cleared payment, every on-time obligation builds a Monaris Score — a real-time financial identity powered by the **Monaris Credit Algorithm (MCA)**. That Score unlocks instant capital, BNPL, and credit backed by income already earned.

Not collateral. Not a bank. Their own cashflow.

Monaris reads your on-chain payment history and optionally blends it with off-chain financial data powered by zkTLS — bank accounts via Plaid, accounting tools like QuickBooks or Xero, and traditional credit data where available. The MCA combines all of it into a single, real-time Score that works whether you have a FICO score, a crypto wallet, or both.

Maria clears another invoice. Her Score updates. An advance unlocks. The cycle breaks.

## What Is Live Right Now

**AR/AP** — Create invoices, send them, get paid. Every cleared invoice builds your Monaris Score. Your payment history becomes financial proof.

**Private Pay** — Send and receive stablecoin payments that are private by default. Counterparties, amounts, and patterns are shielded from public view. One click. Zero gas.

**Monaris Score** — A 0 – 1000 reputation score built from verified on-chain cashflow. The central product that makes everything else possible. **Credit and BNPL are next.**
