Monaris is the first cashflow-backed financial OS for the stablecoin economy.

The world has programmable money — stablecoins like USDC and USDT that move anywhere in seconds, cost almost nothing, and settle instantly. What the world does *not* have is a layer above that money that makes it intelligent, controllable, and useful as financial proof.

Banks have credit scores. Credit scores need bank accounts. Bank accounts need addresses, IDs, credit history, and citizenship in the right country. That system works for about half the world. The other half — 1.4 billion people — earn money, pay people, receive payments, and build real financial lives entirely outside that system. Stablecoin earners, freelancers, remote workers, global teams, crypto-native businesses, emerging market professionals. They have real income. Provable income. On-chain income. But the system cannot see it.

**Monaris sees it.**

Monaris reads your on-chain cashflow history, and optionally your off-chain financial data from bank accounts, credit scoring systems, and accounting tools. It turns all of it into a verifiable financial identity, enforces intelligent controls over your money movement, and unlocks credit based on what you actually earn. The **Monaris Credit Algorithm (MCA)** blends on-chain and off-chain signals into a single, real-time Score that works whether you have a FICO score, a crypto wallet, or both.

## Instant Settlement Liquidity for Everyone

Every word is intentional:

- **Instant** — money moves in seconds, not days
- **Settlement** — final, real, not pending
- **Liquidity** — access to credit, advances, and capital when you need it
- **For Everyone** — not just people with bank accounts and FICO scores

## The core insight

Your on-chain transaction history is the most honest financial record ever created. It cannot be faked. It cannot be doctored. Every payment is timestamped and permanent. A freelancer with 18 months of on-chain income has proof of earnings that is more reliable than a payslip, more real than a tax return, and more current than any credit bureau data.

But Monaris does not stop at on-chain data. Connect your bank account via Plaid, pull in your credit score from Credit Karma, sync your QuickBooks or Xero data — and the MCA combines all of it into one financial picture. On-chain meets off-chain. The result is the most complete financial identity available anywhere.

Monaris is the first company to take that proof seriously, and build a complete financial stack on top of it.

## What Monaris is not

- **Not a wallet** — Monaris works with your existing wallet
- **Not a new stablecoin** — Monaris works with USDC, USDT, and others
- **Not a crypto exchange**
- **Not a bank trying to be crypto**
- **Not a protocol that requires you to lock up collateral you don't have**
