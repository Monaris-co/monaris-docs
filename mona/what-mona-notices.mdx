Mona generates a message on every meaningful financial event. This is the complete reference of what triggers a Mona insight and what she says.

## Invoice events

### Invoice cleared (on time)
> *"Client A paid on time — this is their 4th on-time payment in a row. Strong counterparty. Your Score moved from 640 to 658."*

### Invoice overdue (7 days)
> *"Your invoice to Client B is 7 days past due. Late client payments are one of the biggest Score impacts. Want me to send them a reminder?"*

### Invoice overdue (30 days)
> *"Client B is now 30 days late. Your Score dropped 22 points this week because of this. Would you like to explore an Invoice Advance on a different outstanding invoice to cover your cashflow gap?"*

### First invoice cleared
> *"First invoice cleared. Your Monaris Score just started. 9 more cleared invoices will unlock Established and your first credit access."*

### Milestone invoice (10th, 25th, 50th)
> *"You cleared your 10th invoice. You have unlocked Established — here is what just changed for you."*

## Score events

### Tier upgrade
> *"You just crossed into Established. Your cashflow history is now strong enough to qualify for up to $5,000 in credit. Here is what got you here: 12 invoices cleared, 91% on-time rate, $1,800 in verified volume."*

### Score stagnation (14 days without movement)
> *"Your Score has not moved in 2 weeks. Fastest improvement right now: get one more client to clear an outstanding invoice. That is worth approximately 40–60 points."*

### Score drop
> *"Your Score dropped 35 points since last week. Main reason: Client C's invoice is now 21 days overdue. If it clears this week, you recover most of that."*

### Approaching next tier
> *"You are 80 points away from Trusted. Two more cleared invoices from distinct clients could get you there this month."*

## Cashflow events

### Client concentration detected
> *"Client A makes up 61% of your income this month. If they delay their next payment, your runway drops from 8 weeks to 3 weeks. Worth knowing."*

### Runway warning
> *"Based on your current obligations and inflow patterns, you have approximately 3 weeks of runway. Your next expected payment is from Client B — due in 11 days."*

### Unusual large inflow
> *"You received a larger-than-usual payment today. Your 90-day runway is now comfortable. Want to set up a savings sweep to automatically put 20% of any inflow aside?"*

### Strong cashflow period
> *"Your cashflow looks strong for the next 30 days. You have $12,000 in expected inflows."*

### Inflow pattern change
> *"Client D has not paid in 45 days. They typically pay monthly. This could be a timing issue or a change in the relationship. Worth following up."*

## Credit and BNPL events (Available in V2)

### Invoice Advance available
> *"Your invoice to Client A qualifies for an advance. You could have $4,500 in your wallet in the next 10 minutes. Fee: $120. Want to see the full terms?"*

### Credit pre-approval reached
> *"Your Score now qualifies you for up to $8,000 at 11% APR. You are not committing to anything — this is just what is available to you today based on your history."*

### Repayment upcoming
> *"Your next BNPL repayment of $600 is due in 3 days. Your invoice from Client B should clear before then — I will handle the deduction automatically when it does."*

### Repayment completed
> *"Invoice cleared $2,000. BNPL installment $400 deducted. $1,600 sent to you."*

### Credit limit increase
> *"Your on-time repayment history has increased your credit limit. You now qualify for up to $12,000."*

## Team events (Available in V3)

### Payroll upcoming
> *"Payroll for next week is $24,000. You have $31,000 in confirmed receivables due before then. Comfortable, but worth monitoring."*

### Vendor payment overdue
> *"Vendor B has not been paid in 45 days. This is affecting your Team Score. Want to schedule this payment?"*

### Burn rate alert
> *"Your monthly burn rate increased 15% this quarter. At current pace, your runway is 4 months. Revenue would need to increase or expenses decrease to maintain the same runway."*

## V1 vs V2 triggers

**V1 (Available Now):** Invoice cleared, invoice overdue, tier upgrade, Score stagnation, Score drop — five core triggers.

**V2:** All V1 triggers plus cashflow events, credit events, BNPL events, and conversational responses.

**V3:** All previous triggers plus team-level events.
