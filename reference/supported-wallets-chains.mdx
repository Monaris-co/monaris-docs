## Supported wallets (V1)

| Wallet | Support |
|--------|---------|
| **MetaMask** | ✓ Full support |
| **WalletConnect** | ✓ Any EVM-compatible wallet |

Any wallet that supports WalletConnect protocol works with Monaris. This includes Trust Wallet, Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet, and others.

## What "connect your wallet" means

When you connect your wallet to Monaris:

- **Read-only history access** — Monaris reads your last 90+ days of on-chain transactions
- **Transaction signing** — you approve each outgoing payment individually in your wallet
- **No private key access** — Monaris never accesses, stores, or manages your private keys
- **Disconnect anytime** — you can disconnect your wallet at any time from your dashboard

## Invoice payments without a wallet

Recipients can pay invoices via a browser link without having a Monaris account. They connect their wallet on the payment page, approve the transaction, and the invoice clears. No prior setup needed.
