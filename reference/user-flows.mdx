## Flow 1: Individual user — first session

1. Land on **monaris.co** or **app.monaris.co**.
2. Sign up: **Google** or **email**.
3. Onboarding prompt: **"Create your first invoice"** OR **"Connect your wallet"**.

**If invoice path:**

- Create invoice (amount, client wallet/email, due date, description).
- Send invoice.
- See invoice in **Issued** status.
- Score shows 0/1000 — *"Create and clear your first invoice to start building."*

**If wallet path:**

- Connect wallet (MetaMask / WalletConnect).
- Privacy Router activates automatically.
- Monaris reads last **90 days** of transaction history.
- Mona generates first cashflow commentary.
- Score calculated from on-chain history.
- Cashflow timeline appears.

6. Dashboard shows: Score, recent activity, Mona insight, tier progress.

---

## Flow 2: Invoice payment (recipient)

1. Recipient gets notification: email or Monaris inbox.
2. Opens invoice: amount, sender, due date, description.
3. Clicks **Pay invoice**.
4. Connects wallet or selects saved wallet.
5. Confirms payment.
   - Payment routes through **Privacy Router** (private by default).
   - Sender receives payment.
   - Invoice status → **Cleared**.
   - Both get **private payment receipt**.
   - Sender’s Score: **+20 points**.
   - Mona message fires for sender.

---

## Flow 3: Score improvement journey

- User at Score 200/1000 (Tier C).
- Mona: *"Clear 3 more invoices and you'll reach Tier B — that unlocks credit access."*
- User creates invoices; clients pay; Score rises with each clearance.
- At 450: Mona alerts *"You're 60 points from Tier B."*
- At **510 + volume threshold met**: Tier B unlocked.
- Mona: *"You've reached Tier B. You now qualify for up to $5,000 in credit at 10–14% APR."*
- User can view Credit pre-approval (V2).

---

## Flow 4: Private payment

1. User initiates payment via **Monaris Pay**.
2. **Privacy Router** intercepts by default.
3. User sees: *"This payment is private — only you and the recipient can see the amount."*
4. Optional: user configures privacy for this payment (e.g. "Make this payment public" or "Private with selective disclosure").
5. Payment executes through Privacy Router.
6. Recipient receives full amount.
7. On-chain: proof-of-payment anchored (verifiable, not readable publicly).
8. Private receipt stored in both users’ Monaris accounts.
9. Score updated internally with verified payment signal.

---

## Flow 5: Credit application (V2)

1. User reaches **Score 700+**, **90 days history**, **$5,000+ volume**.
2. Mona surfaces: *"Based on your cashflow history, you qualify for up to [$X]."*
3. User clicks to see pre-approval.
4. Credit terms shown: amount, APR, repayment schedule.
5. User confirms: *"Yes, I want this credit."*
6. Monaris underwrites (Score + cashflow analysis).
7. Credit disbursed to user wallet in USDC.
8. Repayment schedule set: tied to future invoice clearances.
9. Mona monitors: alerts before each repayment date.
10. On-time repayment → Score improves → future credit limit can increase.
