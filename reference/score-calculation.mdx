This is the definitive reference for all signals used by the **Monaris Credit Algorithm (MCA)** to calculate the Monaris Score. For a narrative overview, see [Understanding the Monaris Score](/score/understanding-score).

## On-chain signal weights (V1)

| Signal | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Payment Consistency | High | % of invoices paid on or before due date |
| Income Stability | High | Regularity and predictability of inflows |
| Obligations Coverage | Medium | Reliability of meeting outgoing commitments |
| Client Diversity | Medium | Number of distinct paying counterparties |
| Volume History | Medium | Total verified transaction volume |
| Account Depth | Low | Time since first Monaris activity |
| Repayment Behavior (V2) | High | On-time repayment of Monaris Credit |

## Tier thresholds

All three requirements must be met simultaneously.

| Tier | Score | Invoices | Volume | Credit Access |
|------|-------|----------|--------|---------------|
| Building | 0–510 | 1+ cleared | $0 | None |
| Established | 510–850 | 10+ cleared | $500+ | Up to $5,000 |
| Trusted | 850–1000 | 25+ cleared | $5,000+ | Up to $50,000 |
| Verified+ | Invite only | 50+ cleared | $25,000+ | Up to $250,000 |

## V1 signals (on-chain only)

All V1 signals are derived from on-chain data:

- Invoice creation, sending, and clearance history
- Payment timestamps and amounts
- Counterparty addresses and patterns
- Wallet transaction history (last 90+ days)

## Off-chain signal weights (Available in V2)

| Signal | Weight | Source | Description |
|--------|--------|--------|-------------|
| Bank Statement Consistency | Medium-High | Plaid | Income deposits, balance trends, spending patterns |
| Traditional Credit Score | Medium | Credit Karma | FICO/VantageScore as supplementary signal |
| Accounting Data | Medium | QuickBooks / Xero | Revenue verification, P&L, client relationships |
| Payroll/CEX Verification | Medium | zkTLS | Employment income or exchange history via privacy-preserving proof |
| Repayment Behavior | High | Monaris internal | On-time repayment of Monaris Credit products |
| Behavioral Patterns | Low | Monaris internal | Churn, disputes, defaults |

## Anti-gaming protections

- Volume minimums prevent tier progression from fake low-value invoices
- Invoice count minimums require a meaningful number of real transactions
- Pattern detection identifies circular or artificial transaction flows
- Account Depth rewards sustained presence — time cannot be faked

## MCA update frequency

Real-time. Every cleared invoice, every payment, every relevant event triggers an immediate Score recalculation. There is no batch processing delay. Off-chain data sources refresh periodically based on provider update frequency (e.g. Plaid syncs daily).
