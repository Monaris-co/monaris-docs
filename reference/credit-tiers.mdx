This page is a quick reference for all credit and BNPL product eligibility across Monaris Score tiers.

## Credit eligibility by tier

| Score Tier | Score | Max Credit | APR Range | Origination Fee |
|-----------|-------|-----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Not eligible | 0–510 | — | — | — |
| Established | 510–850 | Up to $5,000 | 10–14% | 2% |
| Trusted | 850–1000 | Up to $50,000 | 6–10% | 1.5% |
| Verified+ | Invite only | Up to $250,000 | 5–8% | 1% |

## Minimum requirements for any credit product

- Monaris Score **700+**
- **90 days** of verified cashflow history
- **$5,000+** in verified transaction volume
- Active invoice activity (not dormant accounts)

## BNPL Pay Later eligibility

| Score | Maximum approval |
|-------|-----------------|
| 510–699 | Up to $2,000 |
| 700–849 | Up to $5,000 |
| 850+ | Up to $50,000 |

## Invoice Advance eligibility

- Monaris Score **700+** required
- Invoice must be from a **verified client** with positive payment history
- Client reliability is checked automatically
- Advance up to **90%** of invoice value
- Fee: **2–3%** of invoice value

## B2B Pay Later eligibility

- **Team Score** required (Monaris Business account)
- Available with Net 30, Net 60, Net 90, or 3–12 month installment terms
- Vendor receives 100% immediately

## Default management process

| Stage | Days Late | What Happens |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| Grace | 1–30 | Reminders only. No penalty. Score unaffected. |
| Late | 31–60 | 2.5% late fee. Score declines. Restructuring offered. |
| At Risk | 61–90 | Account flagged. New credit frozen. Recovery plan. |
| Default | 90+ | Account reviewed. Score reflects history permanently. |

## Important rules

**Credit is never surfaced before eligibility.** No credit offers, no pre-approval views, no APR ranges are shown to users who do not meet the minimum requirements. This is enforced at the system level.

**Score before credit. Always.** Credit products are the last layer of the Monaris stack, not the first.

*Monaris Credit and BNPL products are subject to eligibility requirements including minimum Monaris Score, verified cashflow history, and transaction volume thresholds. Credit products involve risk.
