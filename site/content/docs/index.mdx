Stablecoins solved global settlement. They did not solve cashflow.

People earn in stablecoins, but the moment a payment lands it stops working. It builds no credit history, no financial identity, no access to capital against income already earned. Just a number in a wallet, waiting for the next invoice.

**Monaris is the layer that makes stablecoin cashflow work.** Every invoice, every cleared payment, every on-time obligation builds a **Monaris Score**, a real-time financial identity powered by the Monaris Credit Algorithm (MCA). That Score unlocks instant capital, BNPL, and credit backed by income you have already earned. Not collateral. Not a bank. Your own cashflow.

## How everything connects

Every product is useful on its own, but they share one spine: the **Score**. Each one feeds it, and the Score in turn makes the next one more powerful. The more you use Monaris, the stronger your financial identity becomes.

<ConnectMap />

## The four principles

1. **Measure first, change nothing.** The MCA reads your existing cashflow. No new wallets, habits, or workflows required.
2. **Privacy is the default.** Payments are private by default. Transparency is opt-in, never opt-out.
3. **Score before credit. Always.** Credit is never shown until your Score has real depth: minimum 90 days of history and a qualifying tier.
4. **One platform, modular products.** Use any product alone. All of them feed your Score.

## Get value in 10 minutes

1. Sign up at [app.monaris.co](https://app.monaris.co) with Google or email.
2. **Create an invoice**, or **connect a wallet** so Monaris reads your last 90 days of history.
3. When the invoice clears, your Score gets its first points, automatically.

| Tier | What you need | What you unlock |
