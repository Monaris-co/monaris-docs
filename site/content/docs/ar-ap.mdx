A cleared invoice is not just a payment. It is a data point: proof of a relationship, a payment pattern, a counterparty's reliability, and your own cashflow consistency.

## Receivables (AR), money coming in

- **Professional invoices** with amount, due date, description, and terms. Send to a wallet address or email; the client pays in one click.
- **Instant settlement** in USDC or USDT. No bank holds, no wire fees, no 3-day wait.
- **Automatic Score building.** Every cleared invoice adds points, you do nothing extra.

Every invoice moves through four stages:

| Status | What it means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Issued** | Sent, not yet opened |
| **Viewed** | Recipient opened it |
| **Paid** | Payment initiated |
| **Cleared** | Funds received, Score updates |

## Payouts (AP), money going out

- **Manage what you owe**: vendor payments, contractor payouts, recurring obligations.
- **Schedule and automate** payments, with vendor allowlists so only approved addresses receive funds.
- **Multi-sig approval** for payouts above a set threshold.
- Outgoing payments feed your **obligations coverage**, one of the six Score signals.

## FAQ

**Do clients need a Monaris account to pay?** No. They pay via an email link, connect a wallet on the payment page, and complete the transaction.

**Which stablecoins?** USDC and USDT for V1; more to follow.

**What if a client doesn't pay?** Mona sends automated reminders at 7 and 30 days. Non-payment is the client's behavior, so it does not reduce your Score.

**How do cleared invoices affect my Score?** Each one strengthens your payment consistency and volume history. The exact points depend on your current Score and overall pattern.
