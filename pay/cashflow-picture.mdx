The cashflow picture is Monaris's view of your last 90+ days of stablecoin activity. It turns raw transactions into a structured, categorized timeline that feeds your Score, powers Mona's insights, and gives you a clear view of where your money comes from and where it goes.

## Who this is for

Anyone who has connected their wallet to Monaris and wants to understand their financial position — income patterns, expenses, client concentration, and runway.

## What you see

When you connect your wallet, Monaris builds your cashflow picture automatically:

**Income timeline** — every inflow, categorized and labeled. Who paid you, how much, and when. Recurring income patterns are detected and highlighted.

**Expense timeline** — every outflow, categorized. What you paid, to whom, and how often. Recurring obligations are flagged.

**Client concentration** — Monaris detects when a high share of your income comes from one source. If more than 50% of your income in a period comes from one counterparty, Monaris flags it as a concentration risk. Mona will tell you exactly what it means:

> *"Client A makes up 58% of your income this month. If they delay their next payment, your runway drops from 8 weeks to 3 weeks. Worth knowing."*

**Runway calculation** — based on your inflows, outflows, and obligations, Monaris estimates how many weeks of financial runway you have at your current pace.

**Upcoming predicted income** — based on recurring patterns, Monaris shows what it expects you to receive in the next 30 days.

## How it feeds your Score

Three of the six Monaris Score signals come directly from your cashflow picture:

- **Income stability** — how regular and predictable your inflows are
- **Client diversity** — how many distinct paying clients you have
- **Volume history** — your total verified transaction volume over time

A clearer, more consistent cashflow picture supports a stronger Score over time.

## FAQ

**How far back does the cashflow picture go?**
Minimum 90 days from your connected wallet. Deeper history may be available depending on chain data.

**Can I manually categorize transactions?**
Auto-categorization handles most transactions. Manual overrides and refinements will be available in V2.

**Does my cashflow picture affect my privacy?**
Your cashflow data is processed internally. It is never shared with third parties without your consent. Outgoing payments remain private by default through the Privacy Router.
