## What Monaris Pay does

**Tagline:** Send, receive, and automate stablecoin payments with rules attached.

Monaris Pay reads your existing wallet history, categorizes cashflows, and lets you attach rules and automation to money movement. All payments you send through Monaris Pay go through the **Privacy Router** by default (see [Private payments](/privacy/private-payments)).

**Current status:** In development — V1 scope described below.

---

## Supported wallets (V1)

- **MetaMask**
- **WalletConnect** (EVM-compatible wallets)

EVM-compatible chains only for V1.

---

## What happens when you connect

1. You connect your wallet via the app (MetaMask or WalletConnect).
2. Monaris reads your **existing wallet transaction history** — minimum **last 90 days**.
3. We **auto-categorize** inflows and outflows (income, expenses, transfers, unknown).
4. Your **cashflow timeline** is built and shown in the app.
5. **Mona** reads your wallet history and can generate instant commentary (e.g. concentration risk, runway).
6. The **Privacy Router** is active: any payment you send through Monaris Pay is private by default.

---

## What Monaris reads

- Transaction history (last 90+ days)
- Balances (at read time)
- Counterparties (addresses)
- Stablecoin flows (USDC, USDT)

We do **not** move your funds without your explicit action. Connecting is read-only for history; you approve every payment.

## Privacy

- **Read data:** Transaction history and balances are used to build your cashflow picture and Score inputs. This is stored and processed in line with our [Security & privacy](/reference/security-privacy) model.
- **Outgoing payments:** When you send a payment **through Monaris Pay**, it goes through the **Privacy Router**. Counterparties and amounts are not visible on the public chain in a readable way. See [Private payments](/privacy/private-payments).
