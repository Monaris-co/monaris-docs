Connecting your wallet lets Monaris read your on-chain history and build your cashflow picture. It is the fastest way to start getting insights — and the starting point for private payments.

## Who this is for

Anyone with an EVM-compatible wallet who wants Monaris to analyze their cashflow, build their Score from on-chain history, and route payments privately.

## Supported wallets (V1)

- **MetaMask**
- **WalletConnect** (any EVM-compatible wallet)

EVM-compatible chains only for V1.

## Supported stablecoins

- **USDC**
- **USDT**

Additional stablecoins will be supported in future versions.

## What happens when you connect

1. **You connect your wallet** via MetaMask or WalletConnect in the app
2. **Monaris reads your history** — minimum last 90 days of on-chain transactions
3. **Cashflows are auto-categorized** — income, expenses, transfers, and unknowns
4. **Your cashflow timeline appears** — a complete picture of your financial activity
5. **Mona generates her first insight** — a real observation based on your actual data (concentration risk, runway estimate, income patterns)
6. **The Privacy Router activates** — any payment you send through Monaris is private by default

## What Monaris reads

- Transaction history (last 90+ days)
- Balances (at read time)
- Counterparties (wallet addresses)
- Stablecoin flows (USDC, USDT)

## What Monaris does not do

**Connecting is read-only for history.** Monaris does not move your funds without your explicit action. You approve every outgoing payment individually.

## Privacy

**Read data:** Your transaction history and balances are used to build your cashflow picture and calculate Score inputs. This data is processed according to the Monaris privacy model — it is never shared with third parties without your consent.

**Outgoing payments:** When you send a payment through Monaris, it routes through the Privacy Router. Counterparties and amounts are not visible on the public chain. See the [Private Pay guide](/pay/private-pay-guide) for details.

## How to get started

1. Sign in at [app.monaris.co](https://app.monaris.co)
2. Click **Connect Wallet** in your dashboard
3. Choose MetaMask or WalletConnect
4. Approve the connection in your wallet
5. Your cashflow picture builds automatically

## FAQ

**Does connecting my wallet cost anything?**
No. Connecting and reading history is free.

**Can I disconnect my wallet later?**
Yes. You can disconnect at any time. Your Score data built from your history remains in your Monaris account.

**Do you have access to my private keys?**
No. Monaris never accesses or stores your private keys. Wallet connection is read-only for history and requires your approval for every transaction.
