Invoice Advance is your most powerful financial tool. You have an invoice outstanding. Your client is reliable. You need the cash today — not in 30 days. Monaris advances up to 90% of the invoice value immediately. When your client pays, everything settles automatically.

This is invoice factoring — but instant, Score-based, and stablecoin-native.

## Who it is for

Freelancers and contractors waiting on invoice payments who need working capital now. Small businesses bridging cashflow gaps between large client payments. Anyone with a proven track record of cleared invoices who wants liquidity without taking on traditional debt.

## How it works

```
You have a $5,000 invoice from a verified client — due in 30 days
        ↓
Tap "Advance This Invoice" in Monaris
MCA checks Score (700+ required) and client reliability automatically.
        ↓
Monaris advances $4,500 (90%) immediately in USDC
Fee: 2–3% ($100–150)
        ↓
You receive $4,500 today
Client pays their invoice normally in 30 days as if nothing happened
        ↓
Client pays $5,000 to Monaris
Monaris releases the remaining $500 to you
Advance closed. Score updates positively.
```

Your client does not know you took an advance. They pay their invoice normally on the due date. Nothing changes for them.

## Why 90% and not 100%

The 10% held back is protection. If the client pays slightly less or slightly late, the buffer covers it. When everything clears cleanly — and it usually does — you get the remaining 10% immediately.

## Eligibility

- **Monaris Score 700+** required
- **90 days** of verified cashflow history
- The invoice must be from a **verified client** with a positive payment history
- Client reliability is checked automatically — the MCA uses your counterparty data and any connected off-chain sources to assess the likelihood of on-time payment

## Fees

The advance fee is **2–3%** of the invoice value, depending on client reliability and your Score tier. No hidden charges. The fee is disclosed before you confirm.

## FAQ

**What happens if my client does not pay?**
If the client does not pay the invoice, the advance enters the standard repayment process. Monaris works with you to create a recovery plan. The 10% buffer provides initial protection.

**Can I advance multiple invoices at once?**
Yes, subject to your total credit limit based on your Score tier.

**Does taking an advance affect my Score?**
Successfully completing an advance (client pays, advance settles) improves your Score. An advance that enters recovery affects your Score negatively.

**Does my client know I took an advance?**
No. The client pays their invoice normally. The advance is between you and Monaris.
