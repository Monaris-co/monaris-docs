The Team Monaris Score is the company-level equivalent of the individual Monaris Score. It is built from your business's aggregate cashflow activity — vendor payments, payroll execution, client payments, and obligations coverage — and updated in real time.

## Who this is for

Companies on Monaris Business that want to establish creditworthiness as a business entity, access B2B Pay Later, and build a verifiable financial reputation for their organization.

## How it is built

The Team Score draws from the same six signal categories as the individual Score, applied at the company level:

1. **Payment Consistency** — how reliably the company pays vendors and obligations on time
2. **Income Stability** — regularity and predictability of business inflows from clients
3. **Client Diversity** — number of distinct paying clients at the company level
4. **Obligations Coverage** — how well the company meets its recurring financial commitments (payroll, vendors, subscriptions)
5. **Volume History** — total verified business transaction volume
6. **Account Depth** — how long the business has been active on Monaris

## What the Team Score unlocks

- **B2B Pay Later** — flexible payment terms for business purchases, underwritten by Team Score
- **Higher BNPL limits** — team-level credit access beyond individual limits
- **Business creditworthiness** — a verifiable signal for partners, vendors, and counterparties
- **Priority terms** — better rates and higher limits as Team Score improves

## How it differs from individual Scores

Individual Scores and Team Scores are separate. A team member's individual Score reflects their personal financial activity. The Team Score reflects the company's aggregate behavior. Both can exist simultaneously — a person can have a strong individual Score and belong to a company with a strong Team Score.

## Availability

<Note>
The Team Monaris Score is part of **Monaris Business**, launching in **V3**.
</Note>
