Payroll scheduling automates recurring stablecoin payments to your team. Set the cadence, the amounts, and the recipients. Monaris executes on schedule.

## Who this is for

Companies paying employees and contractors in USDC or USDT on a regular schedule. Remote teams with members in multiple countries who want consistent, automated payroll without manual bank wire initiation.

## How it works

1. **Add team members** with their wallet addresses and payment amounts
2. **Set the cadence** — weekly, biweekly, monthly, or custom
3. **Monaris executes automatically** on schedule — no manual action needed each pay period
4. **Each team member receives USDC/USDT** directly to their wallet
5. **All payments private by default** through the Privacy Router — salary information is not broadcast on-chain

## Features

**Any cadence.** Weekly, biweekly, monthly, or define your own custom schedule.

**Tax exports.** Generate reports compatible with standard tax filing requirements. Export transaction history for compliance purposes.

**Private by default.** Payroll payments route through the Privacy Router. Salary amounts and recipient identities are not visible on the public blockchain.

**Team Score contribution.** Reliable payroll execution strengthens your Team Monaris Score.

## Pricing

Payroll Automation is available as an add-on for **$99/month flat** on any Monaris Business tier.
