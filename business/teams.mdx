Monaris Business is the full treasury operating system for teams that run on stablecoins. If Monaris AR/AP and Private Pay are for individuals, Monaris Business is for companies. Multi-user controls, spend policies, payroll scheduling, vendor management, team-level Monaris Score, and a CFO-level dashboard powered by Mona — all for teams that have moved beyond spreadsheets and email-based approvals.

## Who it is for

Crypto-native companies paying employees and contractors in stablecoins. DAOs managing multi-signature treasury and recurring payouts. Global remote teams with members in multiple countries. Scaling startups that have outgrown manual treasury management. Any company where 3+ people are already using Monaris individually.

## How it works

The natural path is bottom-up. Freelancers and contractors start on individual Monaris. Their clients join to pay invoices. Their clients' businesses start using Monaris AR/AP. Three people at a company using Monaris individually creates an obvious need for shared controls — and that is Monaris Business.

## Core features

### Multi-user workspace
Role-based access for your team: **Owner**, **Admin**, **Member**, and **Viewer**. Each role has precise permissions. Owners set policies. Admins manage daily operations. Members execute within policy limits. Viewers observe without access to initiate transactions.

### Spend policies
Per-user limits, per-category limits, and approval thresholds. Set rules like "any payment above $5,000 requires Admin approval" or "marketing spend is capped at $10,000/month." Policies enforce themselves — no manual oversight needed after setup.

### Vendor allowlists
Only approved wallet addresses can receive outgoing payments. This prevents unauthorized transfers and ensures every payout goes to a verified destination. Add, remove, or update approved vendors from the admin dashboard.

### Payroll scheduling
Recurring stablecoin payroll on any cadence — weekly, biweekly, monthly, or custom. Payroll auto-executes on schedule. Team members receive their payment in USDC or USDT directly to their wallet. Tax exports are available for compliance purposes.

### Multi-signature approval
Transactions above a set threshold require multiple approvals before they execute. Configure the threshold and the number of required signatures based on your team's governance model. This works natively with DAO governance structures.

### Team Monaris Score
Your company's aggregate cashflow reputation, updated in real time. Every vendor payment made on time, every payroll executed reliably, every client payment received and reconciled — these all feed the Team Score. The Team Score unlocks B2B Pay Later, higher BNPL limits, and establishes the company's creditworthiness without a single bank statement.

### Privacy layer
All team payments are private by default, with compliance mode available. Team-level privacy policies can be enforced across all members from the admin dashboard.

### CFO dashboard
A single view for the person managing the money: burn rate, runway, payroll obligations, outstanding receivables, and cashflow forecast — all powered by real-time data and Mona's intelligence.

### Mona for teams
Proactive treasury alerts, runway warnings, and concentration flags for the business entity:

> *"Payroll for next week is $24,000. You have $31,000 in confirmed receivables due before then. Comfortable, but worth monitoring."*

> *"Vendor B has not been paid in 45 days. This is affecting your Team Score. Want to schedule this payment?"*

## FAQ

**How does a team get onto Monaris Business?**
The typical path is organic: individual users bring their companies. When 3+ people at a company are using Monaris, the need for shared controls becomes obvious. Upgrade to a Business plan from your dashboard.

**Can a DAO use Monaris Business?**
Yes. Multi-signature approval works natively with DAO governance. DAOs can manage treasury, vendor payments, and contributor payouts through Monaris Business.

**What is the Team Score?**
The Team Score is the company-level equivalent of the individual Monaris Score. It is built from the company's financial activity — vendor payments, payroll execution, client payments — and unlocks B2B credit products.

**Can team members have individual Scores too?**
Yes. Individual Scores and Team Scores are separate. A team member's individual Score reflects their personal financial activity. The Team Score reflects the company's aggregate behavior.
