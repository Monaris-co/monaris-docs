This is the shortest path from zero to a working Monaris Score. Follow these steps in order.

## 1. Create your account

Go to [app.monaris.co](https://app.monaris.co). Sign up with Google or email.

## 2. Create and send your first invoice

Hit **Create Invoice**. Enter the amount, due date, description, and your client's wallet address or email. Send it.

Your first invoice is now **Issued**. When your client pays, it becomes **Cleared**.

## 3. Get your first Score points

When the invoice clears, your Monaris Score updates automatically. You have just created the first data point of your on-chain financial identity.

## 4. Connect your wallet (optional but recommended)

Connect via MetaMask or WalletConnect. Monaris reads your last 90 days of history and generates your cashflow picture — income, expenses, client concentration, and runway.

## 5. Build toward your first tier

Your path to each tier:

| Tier | What you need | What you unlock |
|------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Building** (0–510) | Start using Monaris | AR/AP, Private Pay, cashflow picture |
| **Established** (510–850) | 10+ invoices cleared, $500+ volume | Score sharing, credit pre-approval view |
| **Trusted** (850–1000) | 25+ invoices cleared, $5,000+ volume | Full credit access, priority terms |

## 6. Watch Mona work

Mona starts surfacing insights as soon as you have activity — who pays on time, where your concentration risk is, how to improve your Score fastest. She uses your real numbers, not generic advice.
