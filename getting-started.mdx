## Land on Monaris

Go to **monaris.co** or **app.monaris.co**.

## Sign up

Use **Google** or **email** to create your account.

## Choose your path

Onboarding prompts you to either **create your first invoice** or **connect your wallet**. You can do both; order does not matter.

---

## Path 1: Create your first invoice

1. Create an invoice: amount, due date, description, recipient (wallet address or email).
2. Send the invoice. The recipient gets it in their Monaris inbox or by email.
3. Invoice status: **Issued** → **Viewed** → **Paid** → **Cleared**.
4. Your Score starts at 0/1000. The prompt: *Create and clear your first invoice to start building.*

When the recipient pays, the invoice clears. You get **+20 points** on your Monaris Score. Your first step toward Tier B and credit access.

## Path 2: Connect your wallet

1. Connect your wallet (**MetaMask** or **WalletConnect** — EVM-compatible).
2. The **Privacy Router** activates automatically for payments you send through Monaris Pay.
3. Monaris reads the last **90 days** of transaction history from your wallet.
4. **Mona** generates your first cashflow commentary from that history.
5. Your **Score** is calculated from on-chain history (where applicable).
6. Your **cashflow timeline** appears.

Your dashboard then shows: Score, recent activity, a Mona insight, and tier progress.

## What you see on the dashboard

- **Monaris Score** — current number, tier (C / B / A), progress bar to next tier.
- **Recent activity** — invoices and payments.
- **Mona insight** — one specific, data-backed comment about your cashflow.
- **Tier progress** — how many points or cleared invoices until the next tier.

---

## The four principles (how Monaris works)

1. **Measure first, change nothing.** We read existing cashflow. You do not need to change wallets or behavior to get value.
2. **Privacy is the default.** Payments through the Monaris Settlement Router are private by default. Transparency is opt-in.
3. **Score before credit. Always.** Credit is never shown until you have a Score with enough depth (minimum 90 days of cashflow history).
4. **One platform. Modular products.** Use any product alone; all of them feed your Monaris Score.
